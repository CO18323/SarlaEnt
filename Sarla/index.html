    About Us

    Established in 1990, Sarla Enterprises played a very dynamic role in Uttar Pradesh’s fledgling printing industry. Over the years, through regular technology acquisitions, exploration of new niche markets and augmentation of efficiency at every level of the organization, Sarla has undergone a metamorphosis – a transformation from just a printing company, into a well integrated, expert and cost-effective solutions provider. With production facilities, that reflect excellence on every front; where technological dexterity, quality of human asset and passion for perfection, we continue to deliver high quality with speed, consistency and cost-effectiveness every time! Today we cater to our high profile clients with a wide range of brand promotional ideas which start from simple printing to security, various kinds of printing on Flex and Synthetic media, all types of UV Printing, to Novelties and Gift items.

    Services

    We provide many kind of printing services such as corrogatio, screen printing, digital printing, etc.

    Offset Printing

    Offset Printing is great for: business cards, letterhead, catalogs, books/booklets, business forms, flyers, brochures, calendars, invitations and so much more. Offset printing offers the best price per piece in the printing industry.

    Digital Printing

    Our digital printing services are a great solution for any quick, immediate printing needs. This service allows for variable data marketing and is ideal for small quantities of printing runs.

    Corrugated Box

    Corrugated boxes are anytime stronger than other usual boxes and it works pretty well for packaging food and any other valuable items. We supply corrugated box in Lucknow which have a wide range of sizes to match your needs with the best quality.
